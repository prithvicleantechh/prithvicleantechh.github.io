About

Prithvi Cleantech

Prithvi Cleantech is a recycling company specializing in lithium-ion batteries founded in 2020. Committed to sustainability, they actively promote education and align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
By recycling batteries and educating communities, they contribute to a cleaner environment and responsible consumption, supporting SDGs such as Affordable and Clean Energy (SDG 7) and Quality Education (SDG 4).
Prithvi Cleantech's mission is to create a greener future while fostering awareness and sustainability.

Initiative

By Prithvi Cleantech

In 2022, Prithvi initiated the establishment of Prithvi Institute, focusing on vocational skill development.
The primary goal was to empower economically disadvantaged youth aged 18-25 with employable skills and connect them to job opportunities or entrepreneurial ventures.
Prithvi Institute also offered valuable practical experience through internships and industrial visits.
Today, Prithvi Institute runs programs nationwide, providing access to entry-level positions and placements in ten major vocations.
In the year 2022-2023, the organization impacted over 500 young individuals, equipping them with the necessary skills to secure entry-level employment or launch their own businesses.

Founder

Ankur Tyagi

Mr. Tyagi envisions a future where he plays a pivotal role in advancing environmental sustainability. He is committed to significantly reducing the environmental impact of discarded batteries by recovering valuable materials, thus minimizing resource depletion.
Our Founder is dedicated to supporting the transition to clean energy technologies and providing sustainable energy storage solutions, contributing to a greener and more sustainable world.
